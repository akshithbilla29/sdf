As a disabled rights activist:- —————————————— Mahender faced many insults and taunts in his childhood. He constantly wishes that this situation should not happen to people like him. While serving the disabled on one side, he is fighting for their rights and self-respect. He gives many advices and suggestions in terms of their career and shows employment opportunities. As District Disable Co-ordination Member:- —————————————————— In the past, Mahender worked for the rights of the disabled as a member of the Vikalangula Hakkula Porata Samiti. At present, as a member of the Hanmakonda District Disable Co-ordination Member, he is participating in various official meetings and working towards solving the problems brought to his attention by the concerned authorities. As a leader of the Disability Employees and Teachers Association:- —————————————————— Mahender founded 'Physically Challenged Teachers Union' (PCTU) in 2010 along with his friends for the employees and teacher issues of Warangal district and served as its General Secretary. He successfully conducted movements at the district and state level to remove fake disabled teachers. He also briefed the officials of Lok Adalat and Human Rights Commission on the issues. He participated in various seminars and voiced the injustices happening to disabled employees and teachers in his own way. He also held the responsibilities as District General Secretary and President of Divyang Udyoga Upadhyaya Sangam and solved many problems. Currently, he has formed an organization called Differently Abled Teachers and Employees Forum(DASF), Joint Warangal Dist. and is fighting on the issues brought to the attention of the forum.
